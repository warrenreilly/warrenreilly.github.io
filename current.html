About KIE's Motorsports


At Kies Motorsports, we bring you the best of aftermarket upgrades for your vehicle. We carry all the major brands the industry has to offer. At Kies Motorsports, we thought long and hard about what we would want as customers, and we built our company around that criteria to provide the best service, products, price and overall experience.


We know going to the dealer to have automotive service performed can be a challenge, so we come to you for all installations, and currently serve NJ, PA, DE, and NY. Please contact us for specifics and special requests. We specialize in BMW and Porsche and are business partners with BimmerTech, Burger Motorsports and P3 Cars, to help find and install the perfect retrofits, modifications and performance upgrades that fit your goals and price point.
